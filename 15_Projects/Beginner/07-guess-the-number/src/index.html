Remaining Guesses: 5

🕹️ How to Play

  1. The game randomly picks a number between 1 and 10.
  2. You have 5 attempts to guess the correct number.
  3. After each guess, you'll get a hint:
    • 🔼 Too high
    • 🔽 Too low
    • ✅ Correct!
  4. If you guess the number within 5 tries, you win!
  5. If you use all 5 attempts without guessing correctly, the game ends.
  6. Click Play Again to restart the game.
